Petroleum products transportation

Oil, gasoline, diesel fuel and other petroleum products are dangerous cargo categories. These are fire hazards products, so transportation of petroleum products is carried out in compliance with the special rules.

Liquid carbohydrates, their mixtures, which are used in the fuel, and special additives are not chemically neutral and can indirectly affect the surfaces with which they come into contact.

It is essential to control the temperature of the freight that is transported. For example, dark petroleum products harden at sub-zero temperatures; therefore, for their transportation, special heating tanks must be used.
